Quick start

How to use the app

  1. Open the Map tab and browse pins for radio stations or places.
  2. Use the search bar to find a specific station or place.
  3. Tap a pin or card to open details, then start playback.
  4. Use the mini player to pause or stop while staying on the map.
  5. Tap the player to open the expanded player for larger controls and station artwork.
  6. Tap the heart to save favorites and return later from the Favorites tab.
Best first checks

If something is not working

  • Confirm that your device has an active internet connection.
  • Try another station to see whether the issue is stream-specific.
  • Fully stop playback and start again from the map or player screen.
  • Reopen the app if remote artwork or metadata appears delayed.
  • Check app permissions in iOS Settings if media or location features are unavailable.
Some live stations depend on third-party stream providers. A station can be temporarily unavailable even when the app itself is working normally.

Map and mini player

The map screen is the fastest way to discover stations and places. The mini player keeps playback controls visible while you continue browsing.

Expanded player

The expanded player shows large artwork, station details, favorite controls, and transport controls for a calmer listening view.

FAQ

Common support questions

Why won’t a station start playing?

First check your network connection. Then try another station. Some live streams can be temporarily offline or slow to respond because they are hosted by third-party providers.

Why is the artwork missing or delayed?

Some station logos are loaded remotely, so they may appear slowly on a weak connection. The app uses a fallback presentation when artwork is unavailable.

How do I save a station or place?

Tap the heart icon on the card or expanded player. Saved items appear in Favorites so you can return quickly later.

How do I add my own photos or videos?

Use the supported Add or Take a Photo actions on the place or station card. If prompted, allow access to Photos or Camera. These permissions are optional and only needed for media features.

Does RadioRoamer require an account?

No account is required for normal use. The app is designed to let you browse and listen without a sign-up flow.

Can I keep audio playing in the background?

Yes. Background playback is supported so audio can continue while you move around the app or switch to other tasks, subject to normal iOS behavior and stream availability.

Why does the map not jump to my location?

Check whether Location access is enabled for RadioRoamer in the iOS Settings app. Nearby behavior and other location-related features depend on that permission.

What should I send in a bug report?

Include your device model, iOS version, the app version, the station or place name involved, and what you expected to happen. Screenshots or a short screen recording are very helpful.

Permissions

Why the app may ask for access

  • Photos: Needed only if you choose media from your library for supported pins.
  • Camera: Needed only if you choose to take a new photo or video inside the app flow.
  • Location: Used for map-related features and optional nearby behavior.
  • Microphone: May be required by iOS when certain media capture flows are used.

You can manage all permissions later in the iOS Settings app.
