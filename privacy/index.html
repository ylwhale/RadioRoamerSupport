RadioRoamer Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 19, 2026

RadioRoamer is designed as a lightweight listening experience for discovering curated places and global radio stations. This page explains what the app may access, how optional permissions relate to app features, and how external services may be involved when you request content.

Information the app may access

  • Photos and camera: If you choose to add your own photos or videos to supported pins, the app may request access to your photo library or camera.
  • Location: The app may request location access for optional map-related features such as nearby behavior or jumping to your position on the map.
  • Microphone: iOS may request microphone access during media capture flows where audio recording is part of the chosen action.
  • Local app preferences: The app stores certain settings and usage preferences on your device, such as onboarding-related preferences and other local app state.

How information is used

  • To play audio and load related station or place details you request.
  • To save app preferences on your device and support the in-app experience.
  • To let you add optional personal media to supported pins.
  • To support optional location-related map features when you enable them.

External services

RadioRoamer can connect to external radio stream hosts and remote image hosts to play live stations and load station artwork. When this happens, those third-party services may receive technical information such as your IP address, request headers, and device or network details needed to deliver the requested stream or image. Their handling of that information is governed by their own policies.

Accounts, selling, and sharing

RadioRoamer does not require you to create an account for normal use. The app is not designed to sell your personal information. Communications with third-party stream or image providers occur only as needed to load content you request.

Data storage

Media or preferences you add within the app are generally stored on your device unless your device or Apple account backs them up using services you enable. RadioRoamer does not ask users to submit personal profiles to use the core listening features.

Your choices

  • You can deny Photos, Camera, Microphone, or Location access through iOS permissions.
  • You can manage permissions later in the iOS Settings app.
  • You can remove the app from your device at any time.

Children’s privacy

RadioRoamer is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect app changes, legal requirements, or clarifications. Updated versions will be posted on this page.
